Founded in 2014, Dr. Delavar Veterinary Pharmacy has become a trusted destination for pet and livestock care in Mashhad, Iran. We provide expert advice and high-quality products to promote animal health and wellness.

Our Founder

Dr. Delavar graduated from Shiraz University in 1994 with a degree in veterinary medicine. He started his career by opening a pet clinic and quickly gained a reputation for excellent care. Over time, he expanded his work to serve both pets and farm animals.

30+ Years of Experience

With more than 30 years of veterinary experience, Dr. Delavar has provided care to a wide variety of animals. He has supported many farmers by visiting farms and treating livestock such as cows and sheep.

Expertise in Livestock Medicine

Dr. Delavar has a strong background in livestock medicine. He offers treatments for large animals and supports farmers in improving animal health and farm productivity.

What We Offer

Our pharmacy provides pet medicines, vitamins, and supplements. We are here to help with reliable, effective solutions.

Our Mission

We believe every animal deserves the best care. Our mission is to make veterinary support easy to access, affordable, and dependable for pet owners and farmers.
